# Evidence Strength and Anti-mind-reading

Goal: prevent the assistant from turning weak signals into confident claims about motives, love, respect, loyalty, or hostility.

## Basic structure

1. Observable behavior: what was seen or heard?
2. Context: what else may affect it?
3. Possible explanations: at least three.
4. Evidence strength: weak, medium, strong.
5. Validating question: what can update the belief?
6. Unsupported conclusion: what should not be concluded yet?

## Signal table

| Behavior signal | Possible explanations | Evidence strength | Better question | Do not conclude yet |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| replies slower than before | busy, stress, avoidance, lower interest, changed routine | weak alone | “最近联系少了，是你比较忙，还是我们之间有什么要聊？” | “他/她不爱我了” |
| avoids one topic | shame, fear of conflict, no answer yet, disagreement, manipulation | weak-medium | “这个话题你是不想聊，还是还没想好？” | “他/她一定在骗我” |
| criticizes an idea sharply | task pressure, risk sensitivity, poor tone, real objection, status defense | medium | “你主要担心的是风险、成本，还是这个方案本身？” | “他看不起我” |
| repeats old grievances | unresolved hurt, need for accountability, poor conflict habit, scorekeeping | medium | “这件旧事现在被提起，是因为它还没被解决，还是因为你觉得模式重复？” | “她就是故意翻旧账” |
| silent after disagreement | processing, anger, withdrawal, fear, contempt, fatigue | weak-medium | “你现在是需要一点时间，还是不想继续这个话题？” | “沉默就是默认同意/不爱了” |
| public contradiction | urgency, lack of tact, power move, misunderstanding, need for accuracy | medium | “你刚才当众指出，是因为觉得必须马上纠正吗？” | “他故意让我难堪” |

## Output pattern

Use this wording:

> 目前能确定的行为是 X。它可能意味着 A/B/C。证据还不足以直接推出 Y。更稳的做法是问一个可验证的问题：Z。

## Stronger evidence

Evidence becomes stronger when:

- behavior is repeated across time and contexts;
- the person confirms the motive directly;
- there are multiple independent signals;
- the behavior has clear cost or consequence;
- attempts to clarify are repeatedly avoided or punished.

Even with stronger evidence, avoid absolute claims unless the data is direct and consistent.
