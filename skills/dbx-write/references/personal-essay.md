# Personal Essay Playbook

Personal writing works when it is specific enough to feel true and restrained enough to avoid performance.

## Core stance

Do not rush to wisdom. Stay with the small friction until it reveals a judgment.

Good personal essays often start from:

- a repeated moment;
- a small shame;
- a private contradiction;
- a sentence someone said;
- a habit that stopped working;
- a choice that felt disproportionally heavy;
- a feeling the author does not fully endorse but cannot ignore.

## Scene before abstraction

Weak:

```text
人要学会接纳自己。
```

Better:

```text
我以前以为接纳自己是一种温柔的状态。后来发现，它更像停止每天把自己拖到审判席上。
```

## Resonance through precision

Readers feel seen when the writing names a pressure accurately.

Prefer:

```text
那种累不是事情太多，而是每件事都像在证明自己还配得上现在的位置。
```

Avoid:

```text
每个人都在生活中承受着压力，我们都需要学会和自己和解。
```

## No fake healing arc

Not every essay needs:

- childhood wound;
- sudden epiphany;
- grand transformation;
- complete reconciliation;
- uplifting ending.

Allowed endings:

- unresolved but clearer;
- quieter boundary;
- a small changed behavior;
- a more honest sentence.

## Personal truth policy

Do not invent:

- trauma;
- family history;
- relationship conflict;
- career achievement;
- income or failure;
- mental health story;
- private conversation.

If the user gives only an abstract topic, write in observational mode:

```text
很多时候，人不是不知道该休息，而是不知道不努力的时候自己还剩下什么价值感。
```

Do not pretend:

```text
那天晚上，我坐在办公室里哭了很久...
```

unless the user provided it.

## Structure

```markdown
# Title

A scene or precise observation.

## 我以前以为

Old frame.

## 后来不对劲的地方

Contradiction.

## 真正让我改变的是

New understanding.

## 这不是万能答案

Boundary.

## 现在我会怎么做

Small practice or stance.

Final sentence.
```

## Tone controls

- More quiet than dramatic.
- More exact than inspirational.
- More honest than polished.
- Humor is allowed when it relieves self-seriousness, not when it dodges feeling.

## Endings that work

```text
我没有变得更确定。我只是少了一点把每个问题都变成自我否定的冲动。
```

```text
后来我才明白，有些事情不需要被解决，它们只需要停止继续审判我。
```

## Endings that smell fake

```text
愿我们都能在不确定中找到属于自己的光。
```

```text
人生就是这样，在风雨中成长，在跌倒中绽放。
```
