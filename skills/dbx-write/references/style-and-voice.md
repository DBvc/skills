# Style and Voice

The target voice is clear, judgment-bearing, and human. It can be sharp, warm, humorous, or restrained depending on the user's material, but it should not become generic motivational prose.

## Default Chinese prose stance

Prefer:

- short to medium sentences with real verbs;
- concrete nouns before abstract nouns;
- judgment with boundary;
- felt observation without melodrama;
- technical terms only when they carry precision;
- occasional metaphor only when it compresses thought.

Avoid:

- official-document tone;
- abstract noun stacks;
- over-balanced paragraphs;
- inflated transitions;
- forced elegance;
- empty emotional uplift.

## Preserve the author

When editing, protect:

- the user's core judgment;
- doubt and hesitation when they are meaningful;
- unusual but effective phrasing;
- domain-specific language;
- roughness that creates trust;
- personal stance and limits.

Do not preserve:

- filler;
- accidental repetition;
- unclear reference;
- AI-shaped smoothness;
- unsupported certainty;
- fake humility;
- fake authority.

## Sentence rules

### Use verbs that decide

Weak:

```text
这个问题会带来一些影响。
```

Better:

```text
这个问题会把成本推迟到调试和协作阶段。
```

### Remove ceremonial transitions

Weak:

```text
值得注意的是，这背后反映了一个更深层的问题。
```

Better:

```text
更深的问题是：团队没有统一的状态 owner。
```

### Prefer specific contrast

Weak:

```text
这不仅是技术问题，更是组织问题。
```

Better:

```text
表面上是组件拆分，真正卡住的是谁有权定义状态边界。
```

### Keep epistemic honesty

Use these when needed:

- "我现在只能判断..."
- "更像是..."
- "如果这个前提成立..."
- "这里我会保留一个不确定性..."
- "这不是结论，只是一个可验证的假设。"

Avoid fake certainty when evidence is thin.

## Rhythm

A paragraph should usually carry one move.

Good rhythm:

```text
AI 让写作变快了，但它没有让表达变容易。它只是把困难从“怎么写出来”，挪到了“你到底想说什么”。以前没有想清楚，会卡在空白页。现在没有想清楚，会卡在一篇看起来很完整、但没有重心的文章里。
```

Bad rhythm:

```text
AI 正在深刻改变写作方式，它不仅提升了效率，也带来了新的挑战和机遇。因此，我们需要重新思考表达的价值，并在这个过程中找到属于自己的定位。
```

## Metaphor policy

Use metaphor as compression, not decoration.

Good:

```text
没有判断的文章像一间样板房：干净、完整、没有人住过。
```

Bad:

```text
写作是一场灵魂的旅行，我们在文字的海洋里寻找星辰。
```

## Technical tone

For technical blogs:

- define terms once;
- avoid unexplained acronyms;
- connect claim to mechanism;
- do not over-personalize technical facts;
- use examples and boundaries instead of slogan.

## Personal tone

For personal essays:

- concrete first, abstract second;
- allow vulnerability but do not manufacture it;
- do not turn every pain into growth;
- leave room for unresolved truth.

## Delete list

Usually delete or rewrite:

- "众所周知";
- "不难发现";
- "值得注意的是";
- "从某种意义上讲" when it adds no precision;
- "可以说";
- "赋能" unless used as a criticized term;
- "底层逻辑" unless a real mechanism follows;
- "抓手" unless writing a business memo;
- "闭环" unless a real loop is described;
- "长期主义" unless cost and feedback are named.
